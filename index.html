InternAI

AI Web App

Project Aim

The goal of InternAI was to simplify and accelerate the internship search for students. We aimed to replace traditional, time-consuming search methods with an intelligent, AI-driven recommendation engine that delivers highly accurate and personalized results in just two minutes.

Problem It Solves

Students often spend hours scrolling through irrelevant internship postings on multiple platforms. InternAI solves this by using AI to understand a student's profile and preferences, instantly filtering out the noise and providing a curated list of opportunities with a 95% accuracy rate, all while supporting multiple languages.
